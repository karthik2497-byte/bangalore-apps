🏙️ About Bangalore

🌳Bangalore is called the "Garden City" of India, known for its parks and green spaces.
💻It's the Silicon Valley of India, home to thousands of tech companies and startups.
☕Filter coffee is a cultural staple. Don't miss the authentic South Indian filter kaapi!
🌦️Bangalore enjoys a moderate climate year-round thanks to its elevation of ~900m above sea level.
🍛Must-try dishes: Masala Dosa, Bisi Bele Bath, Ragi Mudde, and Mangalore Buns.
🗣️Kannada is one of the oldest Dravidian languages with a literary history of over 1500 years.
